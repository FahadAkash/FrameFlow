Features

Everything you need to craft perfect motion

Designed for motion designers and editors who care about how things move.

Core

Editable Value & Speed Graph

Draggable bezier handles on a real value curve. Flip to the Speed view to shape how fast the value moves — accelerate, coast, decelerate. No more guessing what "33% influence" means.

Multi-Property Apply

Tick Position, Scale, Rotation, Opacity — or All. One click bakes the same curve into every checked property at once.

Works on Effects Too

Ease any parameter with 2+ keyframes — Transform effect, third-party plugins, custom parameter names. Select exactly which params to include.

Instant Undo

One-click undo restores everything to before the last Apply. Full history stack up to 25 steps. Also supports Ctrl/Cmd+Z.

Preset Library

Built-in eases — Ease In/Out, Overshoot, Anticipate, Snap, and more. Save your own custom presets and reuse them across projects.

Live Preview

A looping preview of your current ease on each property type — see position move, scale breathe, rotation spin, and opacity fade in real time.

Smooth vs Minimal

Two apply modes — Smooth (~16 keyframes, full fidelity) and Minimal (~6 keyframes, cleaner & lighter). Adjustable count from 3–120.

Non-Destructive

Never deletes your keyframes. Keeps your original endpoints and only adds curve points between them. Your original animation is always safe.

Overshoot & Anticipation

Hold Shift for free handle movement past 0–1 boundaries. Create bouncy overshoot and snappy anticipation curves that make motion feel alive.

Workflow

From keyframes to smooth motion in seconds

No complex setup. No learning curve. Just shape it and apply.

01

Add keyframes to your clip

Set 2 or more keyframes on any property — Position, Scale, Rotation, Opacity, or even effect parameters.

02

Select the clip & open FrameFlow

The panel detects your selection and lights up keyframe indicators for every property. Move the playhead between two keyframes.

03

Shape your curve

Drag the bezier handles to sculpt your ease — or click a preset like Overshoot, Snap, or Anticipate. Hold Shift for free-form.

04

Click "Apply Ease"

One click bakes your exact curve onto the keyframes. Smooth Bezier interpolation on every keyframe, automatically. Not happy? Hit Undo.
